How to Create Your Web Account

Five easy steps to activate your Gateway Specialty & USLI online quoting access.

1
Visit the Portal

Go to gatewayspecialty.usli.com to get started.

2
Click "Start a Quote"

Select the Start a Quote button on the homepage to begin the account setup process.

3
Enter Your Email Address

Provide your email address to begin creating your account. This will be your login username.

4
Verify Your Identity

Select Send Verification Code. A code will be emailed to you by Microsoft — enter it to continue.

5
Complete Your Profile & Go!

Fill in the remaining required fields and click Create Account. Your account is instantly active.

✓

You're All Set!

Your Gateway Specialty & USLI web account is now active. Select any product tile on the "Start a Quote" page to begin quoting online immediately — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
